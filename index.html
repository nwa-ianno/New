While every effort is made to operate tours as scheduled, Hevo Ventures reserves the right to make necessary changes due to unforeseen or unavoidable circumstances such as weather, strikes, supplier issues, or force majeure events.

Itineraries, accommodations, activities, and routes may be modified for operational or safety reasons without prior notice.

Health, Behavior & Participant Conduct

Hevo Ventures reserves the right to decline, refuse, or remove any participant from a tour if their health, behavior, or general conduct:

  • Poses a risk to themselves or others

  • Violates local laws or customs

No refunds will be issued in such cases. Additional expenses incurred will be at the client's expense.

Reservations & Payment

Groups and Individual Travelers (FITs):

  • A 15% deposit is required upon confirmation for bookings made more than 90 days before departure
  • Full payment is due 8 weeks (60 days) prior to departure or by the invoice date
  • For bookings made within 60 days of departure, full payment is required upon confirmation

Cancellation by the Client

Cancellations must be submitted in writing and are effective upon receipt by Hevo Ventures. The following charges will apply:

  • More than 90 days: 10%
  • 90 – 45 days: 35%
  • 45 – 29 days: 50%
  • 30 – 15 days: 70%
  • Less than 15 days: 100%

Some hotels or services may impose alternative penalties. These will be communicated at the time of booking.

Amendments & Supplier Terms

Hevo Ventures uses multiple suppliers, each with their own amendment and cancellation policies. In cases where their policies differ, the supplier's terms will take precedence.

Clients will be liable for any additional costs arising from amendments or cancellations made after confirmation. Hevo Ventures may charge a cancellation fee of up to 100% if the supplier does so.

Cancellation by Hevo Ventures

In the event of force majeure (e.g., war, political unrest, natural disaster, epidemic, closure of transport hubs, etc.), Hevo Ventures may cancel your trip without liability.

We strongly recommend that all clients purchase comprehensive travel insurance that covers trip interruption or cancellation due to unforeseen events.

Tour Pricing

Tour prices are based on prevailing exchange rates and supplier costs. Prices are subject to change in the event of:

  • Government taxes or levies
  • Exchange rate fluctuations
  • Fuel surcharges
  • Airfare or hotel rate increases

All prices are quoted in United States Dollars (USD) unless otherwise stated. Payments must be made at the exchange rate prevailing at the time of payment. Bank charges are borne by the remitter.
